About the Work

"Wilderness" is a setting of a poem written by Jim Morrison's, published in an anthology of the same name.

The Poem

Where are my dreamers
Today and tonight.
Where are my dancers
leaping madly
whirling and screaming

Where are my women
quietly dreaming
caught like angels
on the dark porch
of a velvet ranch
dance dance dance dance
dance dance dance
